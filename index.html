About me

Hello! glad you made it here! 😉

I love collecting figures! Most of my collection consists of anime figures, but I have a few figures from my favourite games as well 😌

I share my collection on my Instagram, where I sometimes have fun posing and dressing them up 😆

That’s me in the picture below, as a Nendoroid! I take pictures of my Nendoroid doll doing fun things with the Nendoroid dolls my two favourite characters - Gojo and Nagi. You can follow our adventures on my Instagram!

I can communicate in English and Chinese (both Cantonese and Mandarin), and I know very limited Japanese 🥰
